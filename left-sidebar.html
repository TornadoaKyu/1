Locus of Control

The degree to which you believe you have control over your life.

External Locus of Control

Their view their successes and failures as someting they can not control. Those people who are pleased because they are "talented, gift, and naturally smart". If they failed, they would say that it was because of bad luck or that they aren't strong enough.

  • Learn More

Internal Locus of Control

They view their successes and failures as something they can contol. (hardwork=success) Those people who are pleased because of their great effort. If they failed, they would accept it and learn from it. Then, try to put in heaps of effort again.

Self-Motivating thoughts

Keep in mind this:

  • "It's up to me to be responsible for every outcome that happens in my life."
  • "It's my choice how hard I work."
  • "It's my choice what I'm going to do next."
  • "I determine my success."
  • "If I want to get this, I am going to work hard to get this."
